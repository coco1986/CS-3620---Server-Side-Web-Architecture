BUILD GUIDES

Building a team in Monster Sanctuary is much more complicated than it seems, at first. Early on, you'll find success by using blitz strategies, but when the game opens up, you'll soon find yourself getting absolutely decimated by enemies, primarily enemy Keepers. Many players have experienced many occasions of frustration with specific battles/fights while playing Monster Sanctuary. Here you will find monster builds that players have found to be successful for those specific battles/fights. Enter in your own builds that you found to be successful and help other fellow players so they too can enjoy the game with less frustration.
